## 🎓 Tips for Best Results

### **For URL Cards:**
1. **Use clean article URLs** - Avoid URLs with too many parameters
2. **Check the preview** - Make sure the right image was extracted
3. **Adjust if needed** - You can change the image using the toolbar
4. **Test your links** - Ensure the article is publicly accessible

### **For Custom Cards:**
1. **Use high-quality images** - At least 1200px width recommended
2. **Keep headlines short** - 8-12 words work best
3. **Contrast is key** - Dark text on light backgrounds (or vice versa)
4. **Brand consistency** - Save your color scheme for reuse
5. **Test readability** - View your card at thumbnail size before posting

### **For Backgrounds:**
1. **Solid colors** - Best for text-heavy cards
2. **Patterns** - Add visual interest without overwhelming
3. **Gradients** - Professional and modern look
4. **Match your brand** - Use your company colors

### **For Text Effects:**
1. **Use shadows for readability** - Especially on complex backgrounds
2. **Stroke/outline** - Makes text pop on any background
3. **Glow effects** - Great for attention-grabbing headlines
4. **Less is more** - Don't over-style your text

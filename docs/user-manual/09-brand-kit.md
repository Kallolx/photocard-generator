# Brand Kit — Your Consistent Identity

## What Is the Brand Kit?

The **Brand Kit** is your personal branding toolkit within SocialCard. It lets you save your logo, favicon, website name, and social media handles so that every card you create automatically includes your branding — without re-uploading or re-typing anything.

---

## Why Use a Brand Kit?

Without a Brand Kit:
- You need to upload your logo every time
- You need to type your website name for each card
- Your cards might look inconsistent

With a Brand Kit:
- ✅ Your logo loads automatically on every card
- ✅ Your website/source name appears consistently
- ✅ Your social media handles are always available
- ✅ Every card you create looks like it belongs to YOUR brand

---

## Setting Up Your Brand Kit

### Where to Find It
The Brand Kit is accessible from the **Editing Toolbar** (the vertical sidebar on the left side of the card preview area).

1. Open any card editor (URL Newscard, Custom Card, Poll Card, etc.)
2. Look for the **Briefcase icon** (💼) in the left toolbar
3. Click it to open the **Asset Manager**

### What You Can Save

| Asset | Description | Used For |
|---|---|---|
| **Logo** | Your brand's main logo image | Displayed on every card's header area |
| **Favicon** | A small icon version of your brand | Used in compact card elements |
| **Website Name** | Your website or publication name | Appears as the source/attribution on cards |

### How to Save Your Brand Kit

1. Click the **Briefcase icon** in the editing toolbar
2. The Asset Manager modal opens
3. **Upload your logo** — Click the upload area to select your logo file
4. **Upload your favicon** — Click to add a small icon
5. **Enter your website name** — Type your website URL or brand name
6. Click **"Apply"** to save and apply to the current card

### How the Website Name Works
- When you set a website name in your Brand Kit, it becomes the **source attribution** on every card
- This means instead of showing the news source's domain, your card shows YOUR brand name
- This is perfect for republishing news under your own publication name

---

## How Brand Kit Works with Different Card Types

### URL Newscard
- When you paste a URL, the system normally shows the original website's name as the source
- With Brand Kit set up, YOUR website name appears instead
- Your logo replaces the scraped logo

### Custom Card
- Your logo and branding appear by default on new cards
- No need to re-upload

### Poll Card
- Brand Kit logo and website name are applied automatically
- Consistent branding across all poll graphics

### Comment/Quote Card
- Your logo appears on quote cards
- Creates professional, branded quote graphics

---

## Changing Your Brand Kit

Your Brand Kit can be updated at any time:
1. Open the Asset Manager (Briefcase icon)
2. Upload new logos or change the website name
3. Click **"Apply"**
4. All cards in the current session will use the new branding

---

## Tips for Effective Branding

1. **Use a transparent PNG for your logo** — This ensures it looks great on any background color
2. **Keep your favicon simple** — It appears very small, so detailed images don't work well
3. **Be consistent with your website name** — Use the same format every time (e.g., always "YourBrand.com" not sometimes "YourBrand" and sometimes "www.yourbrand.com")
4. **Use a square or horizontal logo** — Very tall vertical logos may not display well in the card layout
5. **Set it up once** — The whole point of Brand Kit is to set it once and forget it

# Poll Cards

## Create Interactive-Looking Poll Graphics

The **Poll Card** tool lets you create visually appealing poll or survey cards for social media. While they aren't interactive (people can't actually vote on them), they create a powerful visual that drives engagement and conversation.

> 🔒 **Plan Requirement**: Poll Cards are available for **Basic** and **Premium** users.

---

## How to Create a Poll Card

### Step 1: Open Poll Cards
- Go to **Dashboard → Poll Card**
- Or find it in the sidebar: **Card Types → News & Social → Poll Card**

### Step 2: Set Up Your Content

#### Import from URL (Optional)
- Paste any article URL in the **"Extract from URL"** section
- Click **"Extract"** to automatically pull in the headline, image, and logo
- This is great for creating polls about news topics

#### Upload Your Content Manually
In the **Content** section:
1. **Upload a Logo** — Your brand or publication logo
2. **Upload an Image** — The main card image (optional)
3. **Write a Headline** — The main topic or question
4. **Set Poll Options** — Choose between 2 or 3 options

### Step 3: Customize Poll Options

#### Number of Options
- Click **"2 Options"** or **"3 Options"** to choose how many choices to show
- Default: 2 options (হ্যাঁ / না — Yes / No)

#### Customize Each Option
For each poll option, you can:
- **Edit the text** — Type any text for the option
- **Change the icon** — Upload a custom icon/emoji image
- **Reset to default** — Click the reset button to return to the original option

Default options are:
1. হ্যাঁ (Yes) — 👍 Thumbs Up
2. না (No) — 👎 Thumbs Down
3. মন্তব্য নেই (No Comment) — 💬 Message icon

#### Icon Size
- Use the **Icon Size** slider (12px–28px) to make poll icons larger or smaller
- Default is 16px

## Customizing Your Poll Card

### Image Controls
- **Zoom** — Zoom in or out on the card image
- **Position** — Move the image horizontally and vertically
- These controls help you frame the image perfectly within the card

### Background & Themes
- Use the customization panel to change background colors, gradients, and patterns
- All the same options as URL Newscard are available

### Font Styling
Customize fonts for:
- **Headline** — The main question or topic text
- **Poll Title** — The poll question area
- **Poll Options** — The text on each option button
- **Week/Date** — Timestamp text
- **Footer** — Bottom area text

### Visibility Controls
Toggle on/off:
- Logo, Week, Date
- Poll Title, Poll Options, Poll Icons
- Image, Social Media, Ad Banner

### Layout
- Use the drag mode to rearrange card elements
- Position Logo, Date, Social Media, and Website in any corner

---

## Social Media & Branding

Like other card types, you can add:
- Up to 3 social media handles
- A website URL
- Custom footer text

These appear at the bottom of the card.

---

## Tips for Engaging Poll Cards

1. **Ask provocative questions** — Questions that spark debate drive the most engagement
2. **Keep options simple** — Short, clear options are easier to read at a glance
3. **Use relevant images** — A strong image related to the poll topic makes the card more compelling
4. **Post with a call to action** — When sharing, ask your audience to comment with their answer
5. **Use the "3 Options" mode** — Adding a neutral option (like "No Comment") makes polls feel more balanced
6. **Custom icons** — Upload relevant emoji or icon images for each option to make them visually distinct

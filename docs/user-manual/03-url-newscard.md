# URL Newscard — Creating Cards from Any Link

## The Fastest Way to Create Professional Cards

The **URL Newscard** tool is the core of SocialCard Generator. Simply paste any news article URL, and the system will automatically extract the headline, image, and source information to create a stunning social media card.

---

## How to Create a Card from a URL

### Step 1: Open URL Newscard
- From the Dashboard, click **"URL Newscard"**
- Or go through the sidebar: **Card Types → News & Social → URL Newscard**

### Step 2: Paste Your URL
- Copy any news article URL from your browser
- Paste it into the URL input box at the top of the left panel
- Click **"Generate"** or press **Enter**

### Step 3: Wait for Extraction
The system will:
- Visit the article page
- Extract the headline
- Find the best available image
- Grab the source logo and favicon
- Pull the publication date

This usually takes 2-5 seconds.

### Step 4: See Your Card
Your card appears on the right side of the screen with a live preview. The left panel shows all customization options.

## Understanding the Layout

The URL Newscard page has two main areas:

### Left Panel — Controls
This is where you make all your changes:
- **Theme selection**
- **Background settings**
- **Font customization**
- **Pattern overlays**
- **Visibility toggles**
- **Frame options**
- **Social media & branding info**

### Right Panel — Live Preview
This shows your card exactly as it will look when downloaded. Every change you make on the left is instantly reflected on the right.

### Editing Toolbar
On the left edge of the preview area, you'll find a vertical toolbar with quick-access tools:
- **Image** — Change the card's main image
- **Logo** — Upload or change the brand logo
- **Title** — Edit the headline text
- **Brand Kit** — Apply your saved branding assets
- **Layout** — Toggle drag mode to rearrange card elements

## Choosing a Theme

SocialCard offers multiple themes for URL cards. Each one gives your card a completely different look and feel:

| Theme | Description |
|---|---|
| **Classic** | Traditional newspaper-style layout with a clean structure |
| **Overlay** | Text placed directly on top of the image with a color overlay |
| **Source** | Source-focused design highlighting where the news comes from |
| **Banner** | Wide banner layout ideal for breaking news |
| **Minimal** | Clean, whitespace-focused design |
| **Blend** | Modern gradient-blended design with soft edges |
| **Duo** | Bold dual-tone layout for high-impact announcements |

To change themes:
1. Open the **Theme** tab in the left panel
2. Click on any theme thumbnail
3. Your card will instantly update to the new theme

> 💡 **Tip**: Different themes work better for different types of content. Try "Overlay" for dramatic news, "Blend" for lifestyle content, and "Classic" for general reporting.

## Customizing Your Card

### Changing the Background
1. Go to the **Background** tab
2. Choose a background type:
   - **Solid Color** — Pick a single color
   - **Gradient** — Choose from beautiful pre-made gradients or create your own
   - **Image** — Upload a custom background image
3. For solid colors, you can:
   - Click any of the preset color swatches
   - Use the color picker for any custom color
   - Type a specific hex code (e.g., #FF5733)

### Changing Fonts
1. Go to the **Fonts** tab
2. You'll see separate controls for different text areas:
   - **Week/Day** — The day name text
   - **Date** — The date text
   - **Headline** — The main headline
   - **Footer** — Bottom area text
3. For each text area, you can change:
   - **Font Family** — Choose from Bengali and English fonts
   - **Font Size** — Use the slider to increase or decrease
   - **Font Weight** — Normal, Medium, Semibold, Bold, Extra Bold
   - **Font Color** — Pick any color
   - **Text Alignment** — Left, Center, or Right
   - **Text Shadow** — Add shadow effects (Soft, Hard, Glow, Outline)
   - **Text Stroke** — Add an outline around letters

### Adding Patterns
1. Go to the **Pattern** tab
2. Choose from built-in patterns:
   - Dots, Lines, Grid, Waves, and more
3. Adjust the pattern settings:
   - **Opacity** — How visible the pattern is (subtle to bold)
   - **Scale** — How large the pattern elements are
4. You can also upload your own custom pattern image

### Visibility Controls
1. Go to the **Visibility** tab
2. Toggle on/off individual card elements:
   - Show/Hide the **Day name**
   - Show/Hide the **Date**
   - Show/Hide the **Logo**
   - Show/Hide the **QR Code**
   - Show/Hide the **Title/Headline**
   - Show/Hide the **Ad Banner**

### Adding a Frame
1. Go to the **Frame** tab
2. Enable the frame border
3. Choose the **border color**
4. Adjust the **border thickness**

## The Editing Toolbar (Side Tools)

The vertical toolbar on the left side of the preview gives you quick access to:

### 📷 Change Image
- Click the **Image** icon to upload a new image
- The original article image will be replaced with your upload

### 🏷️ Change Logo
- Click the **Logo** icon to upload a different logo
- Your uploaded logo replaces the automatically extracted one

### ✏️ Edit Headline
- Click the **Title** icon to open the headline editor
- Edit the text directly
- Click **"Apply"** to update the card

### 💼 Brand Kit
- Click the **Briefcase** icon to open the Brand Kit manager
- Apply your saved logos, favicons, and website name
- More details in the **Brand Kit** guide

### 🔀 Layout Mode
- Click the **Move** icon to enter drag mode
- Drag and drop card elements to rearrange their positions
- Position elements like Logo, Date, Social Media, and Website in any of the four corners

## Using AI Content Tools

When you have a card loaded, you can use AI to enhance your content:

### AI Headline Remixer
1. Click the **Title** icon in the toolbar
2. You'll see the current headline
3. Click **"AI Remix"** (the sparkle icon)
4. Wait a few seconds while AI generates content
5. You'll receive:
   - **3 Alternative Headlines** — Different versions of the same news
   - **Social Media Summary** — A ready-to-use post caption
   - **Hashtags** — Relevant hashtags in Bengali or English

### Choosing Hashtag Language
- Toggle between **Bengali** and **English** hashtags
- Click the language switch before generating

### Using the Generated Content
- Click any alternative headline to apply it to your card
- Click the **Copy** button next to the summary to copy it for your social media post
- Click **Copy** next to hashtags to copy them all at once

## Social Media & Website Fields

In the left panel, you'll find fields for:
- **Social Media Handles** — Add up to 3 social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, etc.)
- **Website** — Add your website URL
- **Footer Text** — Custom text at the bottom of the card

These appear on the card footer and help with brand recognition.

## Downloading Your Card

When your card looks perfect:
1. Scroll down below the preview (or look at the bottom of the preview area)
2. Click **"Download"**
3. Choose your preferred format and quality
4. The image saves to your computer

> See the **Downloading & Sharing** guide for more details on export options.

## Tips for Great URL Cards

1. **Use high-quality source articles** — Articles with good images make better cards
2. **Try multiple themes** — The same headline can look completely different in each theme
3. **Customize the background** — Match the background color to your brand for consistency
4. **Edit headlines for clarity** — Sometimes the original headline is too long. Trim it for better visual impact
5. **Use the AI remixer** — Get better engagement with AI-optimized headlines
6. **Save your Brand Kit** — Set it once, and every card you make will have your branding

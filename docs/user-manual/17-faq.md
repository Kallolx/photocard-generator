# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is SocialCard Generator?
SocialCard Generator is a web-based platform that helps you create professional social media graphics in seconds. You can turn news articles, quotes, polls, and custom content into beautiful, ready-to-share cards.

### Do I need design experience?
Not at all! SocialCard is designed for everyone. If you can type and click, you can create professional-looking cards. All the design work is done for you through our themes and templates.

### Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes, SocialCard works on any device with a web browser — computers, tablets, and smartphones. The interface adjusts automatically for your screen size.

### What languages does it support?
SocialCard fully supports both **English** and **Bengali (Bangla)**. The AI tools, fonts, and all features work seamlessly with both languages.

---

## Content & Cards

### How does the URL extraction work?
When you paste a news article URL, our system visits the page and automatically extracts the headline, main image, source name, and logo. It's like magic — no manual work needed.

### Can I use my own images?
Absolutely! Every card type allows you to upload your own images. You can replace the automatically extracted image or start from scratch with your own photos.

### How many themes are available?
Currently there are 10+ themes across all card types, with more being added regularly. Each card type has its own set of specialized themes.

### Can I create cards in languages other than English and Bangla?
You can type in any language and the cards will display it. However, the AI tools (headline remixing, summaries) are optimized for English and Bengali content.

### Is there a limit on card creation?
No! You can create and download as many cards as you want on all plans, including the Free plan. Only AI features use credits.

## AI Features

### What AI features are available?
- **Headline Remixer** — Generates 3 alternative headlines from your original
- **Social Media Summary** — Creates a ready-to-use post caption
- **Hashtag Generator** — Produces relevant hashtags in Bengali or English
- **Background Remover** — Removes image backgrounds with AI
- **Image Enhancement** — Improves person photos for quote cards

### Are the AI-generated headlines accurate?
The AI creates different versions of the same news story — they convey the same information in different ways. Always review AI-generated content before publishing.

### How do credits work?
Each AI action (headline remix, background removal) uses credits. Credits refresh monthly based on your plan. Free plan users don't have AI access.

## Downloads & Sharing

### What image formats can I download?
PNG and WEBP. PNG offers the highest quality; WEBP offers smaller file sizes with excellent quality.

### Do downloaded cards have watermarks?
No! All downloaded cards are completely clean — no watermarks on any plan, including Free.

### Can I use the cards commercially?
Yes, cards you create with your own content are yours to use however you like, including commercially.

### What's the batch export feature?
The batch export lets you process multiple URLs at once and download all the resulting cards as a single ZIP file. It's perfect for creating a day's worth of social media content in one session.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers work best?
SocialCard works best on modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

### My card preview looks different from the download — why?
This is rare but can happen if your browser zoom level isn't at 100%. Reset your browser zoom (Ctrl+0) for the most accurate preview.

### Can I use SocialCard offline?
No, SocialCard requires an internet connection for all features, including card creation and downloading.

### Is my data safe?
Yes. We use secure connections and don't share your data with third parties. Your uploaded images are processed and not stored permanently on our servers.

## Troubleshooting

### URL extraction isn't working
- Make sure the URL is a valid, publicly accessible web page
- Some websites block automated access — try a different source
- Ensure your internet connection is stable

### Images look blurry on the card
- The original article may have low-quality images
- Try uploading a higher-quality image manually

### AI features aren't responding
- Check your credit balance at the top right
- If credits are zero, they'll refresh at the start of your next billing period
- Try refreshing the page and trying again

### The page is loading slowly
- Clear your browser cache
- Try using a different browser
- Check your internet speed

### I can't log in
- Make sure you're using the correct email and password
- Try the "Forgot Password" option
- Clear your browser cookies and try again

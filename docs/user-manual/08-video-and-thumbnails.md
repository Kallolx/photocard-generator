# Video Reel Cards & YouTube Thumbnails

## Two Powerful Visual Tools

SocialCard provides two specialized tools for video content creators: **Video Reel Cards** for animated social media reels and **News Thumbnails** for YouTube and video thumbnails.

---

# Video Reel Cards

## Create Branded Video Cards with Motion

The **Video Reel Card** tool lets you create animated video cards that combine your video footage with branding elements, text, and backgrounds.

### How to Create a Video Card

#### Step 1: Open Video Cards
- Go to the sidebar: **Card Types → Business & Marketing → Video Card**

#### Step 2: Upload Your Video
- The **"Main Video"** upload is always visible at the top
- Click **"Upload Video"** to select a video file from your computer
- Supported formats: MP4, WebM, and other standard video formats

#### Step 3: Customize Your Card

**Theme Tab**
- Choose from available video card themes
- Each theme provides a different layout and animation style

**Background Tab**
- **Solid Color** — Choose a background color from presets or pick a custom one
- **Video Background** — Upload a second video to play behind your main video, or use the default animated background
- **Blur Toggle** — Blur the background video for a professional depth effect

**Branding Tab**
- **Logo** — Upload your brand logo (with show/hide toggle)
- **Favicon / Icon** — Upload a small icon (with show/hide toggle)

**Text Content Tab**
- **Top Text** — Add text above the video (e.g., your brand name)
  - Customize font size (10px–24px) and color
- **Bottom Text** — Add text below the video (e.g., a call to action)
  - Customize font size and color
- Each text element has a show/hide toggle

**Frame Tab**
- **Card Frame** — Add a border around the entire card
  - Toggle on/off, set thickness and color
- **Video Frame** — Add a border around just the video element
  - Separate toggle, thickness, and color controls

### Downloading Video Cards
- Video cards are exported using the special **Video Download Controls**
- The output captures the animation and video content

# YouTube / News Thumbnails

## Design Eye-Catching Thumbnails

The **News Thumbnail** tool creates 1920×1080 pixel thumbnails — the standard size for YouTube and video platforms.

### How to Create a Thumbnail

#### Step 1: Open Thumbnails
- Go to the sidebar: **Card Types → Business & Marketing → NewsThumbnail**

#### Step 2: Add Your Content

**Images**
- Upload one or more images for your thumbnail
- Each image has position controls (drag to reposition)

**Logo**
- Upload your channel or brand logo
- Toggle the **logo background** on/off (adds a colored circle behind the logo)
- Change the **logo background color**

**Title**
- Enter your thumbnail headline text
- This is the big, attention-grabbing text on the thumbnail
- Default sample text is in Bengali

### Step 3: Customize

**Theme Selection**
Two thumbnail themes available:
| Theme | Description |
|---|---|
| **Classic** | Full-bleed image with text overlay |
| **Framed** | Split layout with image on one side and text panel on the other |

**Text Background**
- Change the color of the text background area
- This ensures readability over any image

**Font Styling**
- **Headline** font controls:
  - Font family, size (up to 108px for maximum impact), weight
  - Color, alignment, letter spacing
  - Text shadow options

**Pattern**
- Add pattern overlays to the background
- Choose from built-in patterns or upload custom ones
- Adjust opacity and scale

**Split Style (Framed Theme)**
- **Mode** — Choose between "Border" and "Blend" modes
- **Border Width** — How thick the divider line is
- **Border/Blend Color** — The color of the dividing element

### Preview
- The thumbnail preview automatically scales to fit your screen
- The actual output is always 1920×1080 pixels (full HD)

## Tips for Great Video Content Cards

### Video Reel Cards
1. **Keep videos short** — Short clips work best for social media reels
2. **Use the background blur** — It creates a professional look and keeps focus on your main video
3. **Add your brand name** — Use the top text field for instant brand recognition
4. **Use a call to action** — The bottom text is perfect for "Watch Now" or "Link in Bio"

### Thumbnails
1. **Use large, bold text** — Thumbnails are small on YouTube; big text is essential
2. **High contrast** — Use bright colors that stand out against the image
3. **Keep it simple** — One strong image + one clear message = best thumbnail
4. **Use faces** — Thumbnails with faces get more clicks
5. **Test the Framed theme** — The split layout lets you show both image and text clearly without overlap

# Getting Started

## Creating Your Account

Getting started with SocialCard Generator takes less than a minute.

### Step 1: Sign Up
1. Go to the SocialCard website
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started Free"**
3. Enter your name, email address, and choose a password
4. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 2: Verify Your Email
- Check your inbox for a verification email from SocialCard
- Click the link in the email to activate your account

### Step 3: Log In
- Go back to the website and click **"Login"**
- Enter your email and password
- You're in!

---

## Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your **Dashboard** — this is your home base. From here, you can access everything SocialCard offers.

### What You'll See on the Dashboard

#### Featured Tools
At the top, you'll see the most popular tools:
- **URL Newscard** — Paste a link, get a card
- **Today's News** — Browse live news and create cards
- **Custom Card** — Design from scratch
- **Comment/Quote** — Create quote graphics

#### All Card Types
Below the featured tools, you'll find every card type available:
- **Poll Card** — Survey-style visuals
- **Compare Card** — Side-by-side comparisons
- **Marketing Card** — Promotional and advertising cards
- **Real Estate Card** — Property listings
- **News Thumbnail** — YouTube and video thumbnails
- **Info Card** — Information display cards
- **Islamic Card** — Hadith and religious quotes
- **Wish Card** — Birthday and celebration cards
- **Video Card** — Animated video reel cards

> 🔒 **Note**: Some card types are locked on the Free plan. Upgrade to Basic or Premium to unlock everything.

## Your First Card — A Quick Tutorial

Let's create your first card together! We'll use the **URL Newscard** since it's the fastest way to see results.

### Step 1: Open URL Newscard
- From the Dashboard, click **"URL Newscard"**
- Or find it in the sidebar under **Card Types → News & Social → URL Newscard**

### Step 2: Paste a Link
- Find any news article online (e.g., from Prothom Alo, The Daily Star, BBC, etc.)
- Copy the article's URL (web address)
- Paste it into the URL input box at the top of the page
- Click **"Generate"** or press Enter

### Step 3: Watch the Magic
SocialCard will automatically:
- Pull the article's headline
- Grab the main image
- Find the source logo
- Create a beautiful card for you

### Step 4: Customize (Optional)
- Choose a different **Theme** from the panel on the left
- Change the **Background Color** to match your brand
- Adjust **Fonts** if you'd like

### Step 5: Download
- Click the **Download** button below the card preview
- Choose your preferred quality (PNG or WEBP)
- Your card is saved to your computer, ready to share!

> 🎉 **Congratulations!** You just created your first professional social media card!

## Understanding Your Credit System

SocialCard uses a credit system for certain premium features:

- **AI Features** (headline remixing, summaries, hashtags) use credits
- **Background Removal** uses credits
- **Card creation and downloading** is always free and unlimited

You can see your remaining credits at the top right of the screen. Credits refresh based on your plan.

## Quick Tips for New Users

1. **Set up your Brand Kit first** — Go to the editing toolbar and save your logo and website name. This way, every card you create will already have your branding.

2. **Try the News Feed** — Browse today's headlines and create cards with one click. It's the fastest workflow.

3. **Experiment with themes** — Each theme gives your card a completely different look. Try them all to find your style.

4. **Use AI tools** — The AI headline remixer can give you 3 different versions of any headline, plus a social media summary and hashtags.

5. **Bookmark the Dashboard** — Make it your starting point every time you visit SocialCard.

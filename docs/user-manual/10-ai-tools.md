# AI Tools — Smart Content Creation

## Let AI Help You Create Better Content

SocialCard Generator includes powerful AI tools that help you create more engaging social media content. These tools work with both Bengali (Bangla) and English content.

---

## AI Headline Remixer

### What It Does
The AI Headline Remixer takes any news headline and creates:
- **3 Alternative Headlines** — Different ways to say the same thing, optimized for social media engagement
- **Social Media Summary** — A ready-to-copy caption for your post
- **Hashtags** — Relevant hashtags in your chosen language

### How to Use It

1. Open any card with a headline (URL Newscard, Custom Card, or Poll Card)
2. Click the **Title** icon (✏️) in the left editing toolbar
3. The headline editor opens
4. Click the **"AI Remix"** button (sparkle icon ✨)
5. Wait 5-15 seconds while AI generates content
6. Your results appear below

### What You Get

#### Alternative Headlines
Three rewritten versions of your headline:
- Each one has a slightly different angle or emphasis
- Click any variation to apply it to your card
- The headline on your card updates instantly

#### Social Media Summary
- A short, engaging paragraph summarizing the news
- Perfect for copying and pasting as your social media post caption
- Click the **Copy** button to copy it to your clipboard

#### Hashtags
- A set of relevant hashtags based on the content
- Available in both **Bengali** and **English**
- Toggle the language before generating:
  - Click **"বাং"** for Bengali hashtags
  - Click **"EN"** for English hashtags
- Click **Copy** to copy all hashtags at once

### Tips for AI Remixing
1. **Start with a clear, complete headline** — The AI works better with complete sentences
2. **Generate once, use many ways** — You get headlines, a summary, AND hashtags in one go
3. **Try both languages** — Generate Bengali hashtags for Bangla audiences and English ones for broader reach
4. **Edit after generating** — The AI gives you a great starting point; feel free to tweak the results

## AI Image Enhancement

### What It Does
Available on **Comment/Quote Cards**, the AI Enhancement tool improves person photos by:
- Removing the background
- Cleaning up the image
- Creating a professional-looking portrait

### How to Use It
1. Open the Comment/Quote Card tool
2. Upload a person's photo
3. Click the **"AI Enhance"** button that appears below the upload
4. Wait while the AI processes the image
5. The enhanced image replaces the original

### Best Results
- Use clear, front-facing photos
- Photos with simple backgrounds work best
- The tool is designed for portrait/headshot photos

## AI Background Remover

### What It Does
A standalone tool that removes the background from any image, giving you a clean transparent PNG.

### Where to Find It
- In the sidebar: **Background Remover**
- Or from the Dashboard utilities section

### How to Use It

#### Upload Your Image
1. Click **"Upload Image"** to select a file from your computer
2. **Or** paste an image directly from your clipboard (Ctrl+V)

#### Processing
- The AI analyzes your image and identifies the subject
- A progress bar shows the processing status
- Processing usually takes 5-15 seconds

#### Results
- The background is removed, leaving only the subject
- A checkerboard pattern shows where the background was (transparent areas)
- The result is a high-quality PNG with transparency

#### Download
- Click **"Download"** to save the transparent image
- Use it in your cards, presentations, or any design project

### Tips for Best Results
1. **Clear subjects** — Photos where the subject is clearly distinct from the background
2. **Good lighting** — Well-lit photos produce cleaner edges
3. **Clipboard paste** — Use Ctrl+V to paste screenshots or copied images directly
4. **Use for card creation** — Remove backgrounds from photos, then use them in Quote Cards or Custom Cards

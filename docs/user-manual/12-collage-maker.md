# Collage Maker

## Combine Multiple Images into Beautiful Layouts

The **Collage Maker** lets you upload multiple images and arrange them into professional grid layouts. Choose from various templates and download your collage in seconds.

---

## How to Access It

- From the sidebar: **Collage Maker**
- Or from the Dashboard tools section

---

## How to Create a Collage

### Step 1: Choose a Template
On the left side, you'll see 6 template options:

| Template | Layout | Photos Needed |
|---|---|---|
| **Classic 2×1** | 2 images side by side | 2 |
| **Classic 3×1** | 3 images in a row | 3 |
| **Grid 2×2** | 4 images in a square grid | 4 |
| **Grid 3×2** | 6 images in a 3-column grid | 6 |
| **Modern Mix** | 1 large + 2 small images | 3 |
| **Pinterest Style** | Masonry-style layout with varying heights | 6 |

Click on any template to select it.

### Step 2: Upload Your Images
- Click **"Upload Images"** to browse and select image files
- You can select multiple images at once
- **Or** click **"Paste from Clipboard"** to paste an image you've copied

### Step 3: See the Preview
- Your images automatically fill the chosen template's slots
- Extra images are ignored (only the number needed for the template is used)
- Empty slots show a **+** icon

### Step 4: Download
- Click the **"Download"** button above the preview
- Your collage is saved as an image

---

## Managing Your Images

### Removing Individual Images
- Hover over any image thumbnail in the upload section
- Click the red **X** button to remove it

### Clearing Everything
- Click **"Clear All"** to remove all images and reset the template selection

---

## Tips for Great Collages

1. **Match image orientations** — Use all landscape or all portrait images for the best results
2. **Use consistent lighting** — Images with similar lighting look more cohesive
3. **The Modern Mix is versatile** — Its 1 large + 2 small layout creates visual hierarchy
4. **Pinterest Style for variety** — The masonry layout handles different image sizes gracefully
5. **Upload high-res images** — The collage maintains the quality of your original uploads

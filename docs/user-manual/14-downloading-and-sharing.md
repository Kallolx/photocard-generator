# Downloading & Sharing Your Cards

## Getting Your Cards Ready for Social Media

After designing your perfect card, here's everything you need to know about downloading and sharing it.

---

## How to Download

### Standard Download
1. Look below the card preview area for the **Download Controls**
2. Click the **"Download"** button
3. Choose your preferred format:
   - **PNG** — Best quality, larger file size
   - **WEBP** — Smaller file size, excellent quality
4. The image saves to your computer's Downloads folder

### Video Card Download
Video cards use a special download system:
- The **Video Download Controls** capture both animation and video content
- The export process may take a moment longer than static images

---

## Image Quality

SocialCard exports high-resolution images optimized for social media:
- **Standard cards** — Optimized for 1080p+ displays
- **Thumbnails** — Full 1920×1080 HD resolution
- **All exports** — Clean, crisp edges and accurate colors

---

## Best Formats for Each Platform

| Platform | Recommended Format | Recommended Size |
|---|---|---|
| **Facebook** | PNG or WEBP | 1080×1080 or 1200×630 |
| **Instagram (Feed)** | PNG | 1080×1080 (square) |
| **Instagram (Story)** | PNG | 1080×1920 (vertical) |
| **Twitter / X** | PNG or WEBP | 1200×675 |
| **YouTube (Thumbnail)** | PNG | 1920×1080 |
| **LinkedIn** | PNG | 1200×627 |
| **WhatsApp** | WEBP | Any size |

> 💡 **Tip**: PNG is the safest choice for all platforms. WEBP gives you smaller files with nearly the same quality.

---

## Batch Export (URL Newscard)

For bulk workflows:
1. Open the URL Newscard in **Batch Mode**
2. Paste multiple URLs
3. Apply your theme and settings
4. Export all cards at once as a **ZIP file**
5. The ZIP contains all your cards, ready to upload

---

## Sharing Tips

### Social Media Posts
1. Download your card
2. Open your social media app or website
3. Create a new post
4. Upload the card image
5. **Pro tip**: Use the AI-generated **Social Media Summary** as your post caption
6. Add the AI-generated **Hashtags** for better reach

### WhatsApp / Messaging
1. Download the card (WEBP format is great for messaging — smaller file size)
2. Open WhatsApp or your messaging app
3. Share the image directly

### Email & Newsletters
1. Download as PNG for best email compatibility
2. Insert the image into your email or newsletter template

---

## Troubleshooting Downloads

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| **Download doesn't start** | Make sure pop-ups aren't blocked in your browser |
| **Image looks blurry** | Try downloading in PNG format instead of WEBP |
| **Colors look different** | This can happen on different screens — the downloaded file is accurate |
| **File is too large** | Use WEBP format for smaller file sizes |
| **Can't find the downloaded file** | Check your browser's Downloads folder |

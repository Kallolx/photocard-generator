# Quote & Comment Cards

## Turn Any Quote into a Visual Masterpiece

The **Comment/Quote Card** tool transforms any statement, quote, or social media comment into a beautifully designed graphic card. Perfect for sharing inspirational quotes, expert opinions, or notable comments.

> 🔒 **Plan Requirement**: Quote Cards are available for **Basic** and **Premium** users.

---

## How to Create a Quote Card

### Step 1: Open Quote Cards
- Go to **Dashboard → Comment/Quote**
- Or find it in the sidebar: **Card Types → News & Social → Comment/Quote**

### Step 2: Add Your Content

#### Upload a Logo
- Click the **Upload Logo** button in the uploads section
- Browse and select your brand logo

#### Upload a Person's Photo
- Click the **Upload Image** button
- Select a photo of the person being quoted
- The photo will appear on the card

#### AI Image Enhancement
After uploading a person's photo:
- An **"AI Enhance"** button appears below the upload
- Click it to automatically improve and clean up the photo
- The AI removes the background and enhances the image quality

#### Write the Quote
- In the **Content** section, find the **"Quote / Comment"** text area
- Type or paste the quote text
- Bengali text is fully supported

#### Add the Person's Name
- Enter the person's name in the **"Person Name"** field
- This appears below the quote on the card

## Choosing a Card Style

Quote Cards come in 5 distinctive styles:

| Style | Description | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Classic** | Traditional centered quote with circular person image below | General quotes, expert opinions |
| **Grid** | Modern grid layout with image and text side by side | Social media comments, testimonials |
| **Split** | Bold split design with image on one side, text on the other | Interview excerpts, feature quotes |
| **Portrait** | Full-portrait layout with text overlay and highlight feature | Dramatic statements, keynote quotes |
| **Quote Frame** | Elegant framed design with decorative quote marks | Inspirational quotes, book excerpts |

To change styles:
1. Open the **Theme** tab in the customization panel
2. Click on any style thumbnail
3. Your card updates instantly

---

## Image Position Controls

After uploading a person's photo, you can fine-tune how it appears:

### Horizontal Position
- Use the slider to move the image left or right
- Range: -150 to +150

### Vertical Position
- Use the slider to move the image up or down
- Range: -150 to +150

### Scale
- Use the slider to zoom in or out
- Range: 30% to 200%

### Reset
- Click **"Reset Position"** to return to the default

## Word Highlighting (Portrait & Quote Frame Styles)

For the **Portrait** and **Quote Frame** styles, you get a special **Word Highlighting** feature:

### How to Highlight Words
1. In the preview area, look for the **Highlight** tool on the left toolbar
2. Click it to open the word selector
3. You'll see every word from your quote displayed as individual buttons
4. **Tap any word** to select/deselect it for highlighting
5. Selected words get a colored background box on the card
6. Click **"Apply Changes"** to save your selection

### Toggle Highlights
- Use the **"Show highlights"** switch to turn the highlight effect on or off without losing your word selection

### Card Background Color
- For Portrait and Quote Frame styles, you also get a **Background Color** picker in the left toolbar
- Click the **Palette** icon to change the card's inner background color

## Customization Options

### Background
- Change the overall card background color, gradient, or image
- Each card style has its own default background that changes when you switch themes

### Font Styling
Customize multiple text areas:
- **Comment Text** — The main quote text (font, size, weight, color, alignment)
- **Person Name** — The speaker's name
- **Person Role** — Their title or role (if visible)

### Visibility Controls
Toggle these elements on or off:
- Logo
- Date
- Comment Text
- Person Name
- Person Role
- Image
- Social Media
- Ad Banner

## Tips for Great Quote Cards

1. **Keep quotes short** — 2-4 sentences work best visually
2. **Use high-quality person photos** — Clear headshots work better than full-body shots
3. **Try AI Enhancement** — It can dramatically improve photo quality by removing backgrounds
4. **Use word highlighting** — Draw attention to the most important part of the quote
5. **Match the style to the content** — Use Portrait for dramatic quotes, Grid for casual comments, Classic for professional statements
6. **Adjust the image position** — Fine-tuning the horizontal, vertical, and scale makes a huge difference in how professional the card looks

# Account Settings & Security

## Managing Your SocialCard Account

Your account settings let you manage your profile, security, billing, and integrations all in one place.

---

## How to Access Settings

1. Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar menu
2. Or navigate directly to the Settings page

---

## Settings Sections

### Profile
Manage your personal information:
- **Name** — Update your display name
- **Email** — Your registered email address
- **Profile picture** — Your avatar

### Security
Keep your account safe:
- **Change Password** — Update your password regularly
- **Session Management** — See where you're logged in

### Plan & Billing
Manage your subscription:
- **Current Plan** — See which plan you're on
- **Upgrade/Downgrade** — Change your plan
- **Billing History** — View past payments

### Developer API
For technical users:
- **API Key** — Generate or view your API key
- **Usage Stats** — See how many API calls you've made
- **Usage Limit** — Your plan's API call limit

### Integrations
Connect external services:
- **WordPress** — Connect your WordPress site for direct publishing
- **Facebook** — Connect your Facebook Page for sharing

---

## Account Security Tips

1. **Use a strong password** — Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
2. **Don't share your account** — Each user should have their own account
3. **Log out on shared devices** — Always log out when using public computers
4. **Keep your API key private** — Never share your Developer API key publicly

---

## Account Status

On the settings page, you can always see:
- Your **name** and **plan**
- A green indicator showing your account is **active**
- Quick access to all settings sections

# Islamic / Hadith Cards

## Beautiful Cards for Islamic Content

The **Islamic Card** tool is specially designed for creating beautiful graphics featuring Hadith, Quranic verses, and Islamic quotes. It comes with elegant themes that respect the sacred nature of the content.

> 🔒 **Plan Requirement**: Islamic Cards are available for **Basic** and **Premium** users.

---

## How to Create an Islamic Card

### Step 1: Open Islamic Cards
- Go to **Dashboard → Islamic Card**
- Or find it in the sidebar: **Card Types → Personal & Creative → Islamic Card**

### Step 2: Enter Your Content

The left panel has organized tabs for easy navigation:

#### Content Tab
This is where you enter the main text:

| Field | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Narrator** | Who said or reported the hadith | রাসুলুল্লাহ ﷺ বলেছেন: |
| **Hadis Text** | The main hadith or quote text | Enter the full text here |
| **Source / Reference** | Where the hadith comes from | সহীহ বুখারী: ৫৩৫৩ |

#### Uploads Tab
- **Upload Logo** — Add your organization's logo
- **Remove Logo** — Click to remove an uploaded logo

## Choosing a Theme

Islamic Cards come with 2 beautiful themes:

| Theme | Description |
|---|---|
| **Classic Islamic** | Elegant dark design with golden accents and Islamic geometric elements |
| **Scenic Split** | Nature-inspired split layout with scenic backgrounds |

To switch:
1. Go to the **Theme** tab
2. Click on a theme thumbnail
3. The card transforms instantly

More themes are being added regularly.

## Font Customization

The **Fonts** tab gives you precise control over three text areas:

### Narrator Text
- Font family (Noto Serif Bengali, Hind Siliguri, Tiro Bangla)
- Size (10px–30px)
- Weight (Normal, Medium, Semibold, Bold, Extra Bold)
- Color with hex input
- Alignment (Left, Center, Right)

### Hadis Text
- Same controls as above
- Size range: 14px–48px (larger for emphasis)
- This is the main text, so it gets the most visual prominence

### Source Text
- Same controls
- Size range: 10px–24px
- Typically smaller and in a different color for visual hierarchy

## Visibility Controls

In the **Visibility** tab, toggle elements on or off:
- **Logo** — Show/hide the organization logo
- **Narrator** — Show/hide the narrator line
- **Source** — Show/hide the source reference
- **Social Footer** — Show/hide the social media footer bar

---

## Social Footer

The Islamic Card has a dedicated **Footer** system for adding social media handles and contact information.

### Setting Up the Footer

1. Go to the **Footer** tab
2. Toggle **"Show Footer"** to ON
3. Add footer items (up to 3):
   - **Facebook** — Your Facebook page
   - **Instagram** — Your Instagram handle
   - **YouTube** — Your YouTube channel
   - **Twitter/X** — Your Twitter handle
   - **TikTok** — Your TikTok profile
   - **Website** — Your website URL
   - **Text** — Any custom text

### Footer Styling Options
- **Opacity** — Control how visible the footer is (10%–100%)
- **Icon Style** — Choose between "White" (all white icons) or "Colored" (brand-colored icons)
- **Text Color** — Set the footer text color

### Managing Footer Items
- **Edit** — Click the pencil icon on any item to modify it
- **Delete** — Click the X icon to remove an item
- Add, edit, or remove items at any time

## Tips for Beautiful Islamic Cards

1. **Use the Classic theme for formal content** — Its dark, elegant design suits serious religious text
2. **Keep the hadith text concise** — Longer texts may reduce font size significantly
3. **Use the golden accent color** — The default gold (#c9a84c) for narrator and source text creates a respectful, premium look
4. **Add your mosque or organization logo** — It builds recognition and trust
5. **Use the footer wisely** — Add your Facebook page or YouTube channel so viewers know where to find more content
6. **Center-align for symmetry** — Islamic calligraphy and text traditionally looks best centered
7. **Adjust font weights** — Use bold for the hadith text and lighter weights for the narrator and source

# Custom Cards — Design from Scratch

## Build Your Own Cards with Full Control

The **Custom Card** tool gives you a blank canvas to create social media graphics entirely from your own content. You provide the headline, image, and logo — and SocialCard gives you professional-grade design tools to make it look amazing.

> 🔒 **Plan Requirement**: Custom Cards are available for **Basic** and **Premium** users.

---

## Getting Started

1. Go to **Dashboard → Custom Card** (or find it in the sidebar under **Card Types → Personal & Creative**)
2. You'll see the split-panel layout:
   - **Left side** — Your controls and customization panel
   - **Right side** — Live preview of your card

---

## Adding Your Content

### Upload Your Logo
1. In the left panel, find the **Upload Logo** area
2. Click it to browse your computer for a logo image
3. Once uploaded, it appears on the card automatically

### Upload Your Image
1. Find the **Upload Image** area
2. Click to browse and select your main card image
3. The image fills the card's photo area

### Import from URL (Optional)
You can also import content from a web link:
1. Find the **URL input** at the top
2. Paste any article or webpage URL
3. Click **"Extract"**
4. The system will pull the headline, image, and logo from the page

### Write Your Headline
- The card starts with a sample headline in Bengali
- Type your own headline to replace it
- It updates on the preview instantly

---

## Choosing a Theme

Custom Cards come with 5 unique themes:

| Theme | Description |
|---|---|
| **Classic** | Traditional card layout with image at top, text at bottom |
| **Modern** | Contemporary design with flexible element positioning |
| **Modern 2** | Updated modern theme with a center favicon slot |
| **Vertical** | Tall, portrait-oriented layout ideal for Instagram Stories |
| **Minimal** | Clean, stripped-down design with maximum whitespace |

To switch themes:
1. Open the **Theme** tab in the customization panel
2. Click any theme thumbnail
3. Your card instantly transforms

## Customizing Your Card

### Background
- **Solid Colors** — Choose from presets or pick any custom color
- **Gradients** — Beautiful two-color transitions
- **Image Backgrounds** — Upload your own background image

### Font Styling
Customize text for different areas:
- **Week/Day** — Day name display
- **Date** — Date display
- **Headline** — Your main text
- **Footer** — Bottom section

For each, you can change:
- Font family (Bengali and English options)
- Size, weight, color
- Alignment (left, center, right)
- Text shadows and stroke effects

### Patterns
Add visual texture with overlay patterns:
- Choose from 10+ built-in geometric patterns
- Upload custom patterns
- Adjust opacity and scale

### Ad Banner
- Upload a banner image for promotions or advertising
- Adjust zoom and position within the banner area

### Frame
- Add a border frame around the entire card
- Choose the border color and thickness

### Visibility Controls
Toggle individual elements on/off:
- Day name
- Date
- Logo
- Title/Headline
- Ad Banner

## Rearranging Card Elements

Custom Cards support **drag-and-drop layout customization**:

1. Click the **Move** icon in the editing toolbar (left side of preview)
2. **Drag Mode** activates — you'll see element handles appear
3. Drag elements to different positions:
   - **Top Left** — Logo, Date, Social Media, or Website
   - **Top Right** — Logo, Date, Social Media, or Website
   - **Bottom Left** — Logo, Date, Social Media, or Website
   - **Bottom Right** — Logo, Date, Social Media, or Website

4. Click the **Move** icon again to exit drag mode

> 💡 The Vertical and Minimal themes have their own specialized layout systems optimized for their unique designs.

---

## Social Media & Branding

### Adding Social Media Handles
In the social media section:
1. Select a platform (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, TikTok, etc.)
2. Enter your username or handle
3. You can add up to 3 social media entries
4. They appear in the card footer

### Adding a Website
- Select **"Website"** as the platform type
- Enter your website URL
- It displays with a globe icon on the card

### Adding Custom Footer Text
- Select **"Text"** as the platform type
- Enter any text you want in the footer area

---

## State Saving

Don't worry about losing your work! Custom Card automatically saves your progress:
- Your uploaded images, text, theme selection, font settings, and layout are all saved locally
- If you navigate away and come back, your work will be restored
- When you leave the page completely, the saved state is cleared for a fresh start

---

## Tips for Great Custom Cards

1. **Start with a clear headline** — Keep it short and impactful (2-3 lines maximum)
2. **Use high-resolution images** — Blurry images make your card look unprofessional
3. **Match background to brand** — Use your brand colors for consistency
4. **Try the Vertical theme** — It's perfect for Instagram Stories and Reels
5. **Use the Minimal theme for quotes** — Less is more when the text is the focus
6. **Set up your Brand Kit** — Your logo and social handles will auto-populate on every new card

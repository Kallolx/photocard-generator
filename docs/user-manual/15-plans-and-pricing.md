## Upgrading Your Plan

### From the App
1. You'll see an **"Upgrade"** prompt whenever you try to access a locked feature
2. Click **"Upgrade Now"** on the prompt
3. Choose your plan

3. Click to upgrade or change plans

## Understanding Credits

AI features use a credit system:
- **Each AI headline remix** = credits used
- **Each background removal** = credits used
- Credits refresh monthly
- Basic plans get a standard credit allowance
- Premium plans get a larger credit allowance
- Enterprise plans get unlimited credits

Your remaining credits are always visible at the top right of the screen.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I try premium features before paying?**
A: The URL Newscard tool is available on the Free plan, so you can experience the core card creation workflow before upgrading.

**Q: What happens if I run out of credits?**
A: AI features will be temporarily unavailable until your credits refresh at the start of your next billing cycle.

**Q: Can I downgrade my plan?**
A: Yes, contact us and we'll adjust your plan. You keep access to your current plan until the end of your billing period.

**Q: Do my cards have watermarks on the free plan?**
A: No, all cards are watermark-free on every plan.

**Q: Is there a limit on how many cards I can create?**
A: No! Card creation and downloading is unlimited on all plans. Only AI features use credits.
